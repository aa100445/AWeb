#1 Trending

On Spotify

The Number 1 artist on Spotify, proudly donning their #1 in the world badge on their 'About' page, is the one-man band The Weeknd.

Abel Makkonen Tesfaye, known professionally as the Weeknd, is a Canadian singer and songwriter. Known for his sonic versatility and dark lyricism, his music explores escapism, romance, and melancholia, and is often inspired by personal experiences. His most streamed song on Spotify is 'Blinding Lights', with over 3 billion people listening to the song monthly. The Weeknd has about 94.27 million monthly listeners.

The Number 2 artist on Spotify is Ed Sheeran, with about 82.50 million monthly listeners.

On YouTube

The YouTube channel with the most subscribers is the Indian film production company and music record label T-Series. With over 233 million subscribers on YouTube, T-Series is primarily known for Bollywood music sountracks and Indi-Pop music.

As of 2014, T-Series is India's largest music record label, followed by Sony Music India and Zee Music. The channel has a total of 212 billion views as of 10 January 2023.

The Number 2 channel of YouTube is YouTube Movies itself, with about 158 million subscribers.
